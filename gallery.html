01 — Dark Coffee House

Espresso browns, warm amber accents, Playfair Display serif headings. Glassmorphism cards, floating coffee beans.

02 — Glassmorphism

Purple→blue→teal gradient mesh, frosted glass cards, floating orbs, Sora headings. Vibrant and modern.

Glass • Gradient • Vibrant

03 — Minimalist Scandinavian

Off-white, warm grey, terracotta accent. Ultra-clean, numbered features, generous whitespace. Cormorant Garamond.

Clean • Warm • Serif

04 — Neon Cyberpunk

Pure black, neon cyan/pink/purple. Glitch text, scanlines, grid overlay. Space Grotesk + JetBrains Mono.

Neon • Bold • Retro-future

05 — Botanical Warm

Sage green, warm brown, dusty rose. Leaf decorations, organic shapes, parallax. Libre Baskerville serif.

Nature • Warm • Organic

06 — Luxury Dark + Gold

Deep black, rich gold accents, floating gold particles. Cormorant serif headings, shimmer buttons. Premium feel.

Luxury • Gold • Premium

07 — Aurora Mesh

Deep navy with animated gradient blobs in purple/blue/teal/pink. Morphing mesh, gradient text. Plus Jakarta Sans.

Aurora • Animated • Bold

08 — Editorial Monochrome

Black & white magazine style, vermillion red accent. Oversized Fraunces serif, pull-quote testimonials. Paper texture.

Editorial • Bold • Magazine

09 — Retro Pixel

Dark purple, electric blue/green/yellow. Pixel art coffee cup, CRT scanlines, star field. Press Start 2P font.

Pixel • Arcade • Fun

10 — Neumorphic Depth

Soft grey with embossed/debossed 3D effects, coral accent. Light/dark toggle, parallax hover. Manrope headings.

3D • Soft • Tactile
